# 🇲🇾 Malaysia Vehicle Registration Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze vehicle registration trends in Malaysia using official government open data from Data.gov.my.

The dashboard explores registration patterns by state, vehicle type, manufacturer, fuel type, and consumer preferences. It also analyzes historical trends in electric and hybrid vehicle adoption to provide meaningful insights into Malaysia's evolving automotive market.

---

## Project Highlights

- Developed two interactive Power BI dashboards.
- Combined and transformed multiple government datasets using Power Query.
- Designed KPI cards and interactive filters for data exploration.
- Visualized geographic, temporal, and categorical trends.
- Applied data storytelling techniques to communicate actionable insights.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze vehicle registration patterns across different Malaysian states.
- Compare vehicle registrations by manufacturer and vehicle type.
- Examine fuel usage trends and the adoption of alternative fuel vehicles.
- Identify consumer preferences through vehicle colour and registration patterns.
- Visualize historical registration trends to support transportation-related decision-making.

## Dataset

Source:
**Data.gov.my – Malaysia Open Government Data**

Datasets used:

- cars_2026.csv
- motorcycles_2026.csv
- registrations_type_fuel.csv

The datasets were cleaned, transformed, and combined using Power Query before being visualized in Power BI.

# Key Insights

- Kuala Lumpur recorded the highest number of vehicle registrations.
- Motorcycles were the most frequently registered vehicle type.
- Yamaha was the leading manufacturer by registration volume.
- Petrol remained the dominant fuel type across Malaysia.
- Electric and hybrid vehicle registrations showed steady growth between 2020 and 2026.
- Malaysian consumers preferred neutral vehicle colours such as grey, blue, and white.
